Competitive Edge

When you make an offer to a Hug Agency candidate you also open up the opportunity for them to receive hundreds or thousands of pounds of free additional wealth. When reaching the critical make or break offer stage with candidates, this can only help to position you ahead of the competition.

Staff loyalty and retention

When you consider the average recruitment agency introduction fee equates to a significant percentage of an annual salary, having a large proportion of that gifted back to your new hire is an amazing gesture you are making as a new employer.

This will naturally help to build a bond of loyalty between the employer and employee from day one. It also encourages the employee to stay for at least two years, at which point they are more likely to be settled and comfortable within your organisation.
